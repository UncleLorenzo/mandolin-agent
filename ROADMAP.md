# Roadmap

v0.1 is the core — working, tested, and honest about itself. Where it's headed:

## Near term
- **Always-on gateway** — reach the agent from Telegram / Discord / Slack. It lives on your
  server, not your laptop.
- **Per-path write scoping** — gate writes by location, not just by capability class.
- **`npx mando`** — a published build so you can run it without cloning.

## Later
- **Semantic recall** alongside keyword search over memory.
- **Signed skills** — cryptographic provenance for instincts shared between machines.
- **More providers** out of the box.

The two things that won't change: it learns *you*, and it earns every action before it
takes it.
